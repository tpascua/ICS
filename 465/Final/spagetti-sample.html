Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 6-8 oz. spaghetti
  • 2-4 cloves garlic (however much you want)
  • a handful of cherry tomatoes (however much you want)
  • 1/4 to 1/2 cup olive oil
  • salt and pepper
  • 2-5 basil leaves (optional)
  • grated parmesean cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Halve the tomatoes and mince the garlic finely.
  2. Put the tomatoes, minced garlic, and basil into a bowl with some olive oil and salt/pepper it to your preference. Let it sit for a while.
  3. In a pot, get water boiling, then reduce the heat slightly and add your spaghetti for 3-5 minutes. Make sure to salt your water or you get bland spaghetti.
  4. When the pasta is done, drain the spaghetti then add it to the bowl with the tomatoes in it.
  5. Mix it around, add cheese, eat it by yourself or someone else.
